Domain Transfer using GAN

Summer Research Intern, Summer 2018

Taipei, Taiwan

This summer internship gives me a peek to computer vision research. Throught the summer, I created a program to transfer human face photos to human face skecthes. Conditional generative adversarial network is used to cope with the lack of training data. Principal component analysis is used to speed up computation. Besides research, I attended group meetings and presented paper reviews. With this experience, I am able to delve more deeply into the field of computer vision.
